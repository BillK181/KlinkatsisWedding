Honorable Mentions

  • Catalina Island: Known for its crystal-clear waters, wildlife, and the charming seaside town of Avalon, with popular activities like snorkeling, hiking, and glass-bottom boat tours.
  • Malibu: Known for its stunning beaches, surf culture, and luxurious oceanfront homes.
  • Temecula Valley: Known for its nearly 50 wineries, rolling hills, and Mediterranean-like climate
  • Pasadena: Known for the Tournament of Roses Parade and Rose Bowl, the city has attractions like architectural gems, museums, and a vibrant food/shopping scene.
